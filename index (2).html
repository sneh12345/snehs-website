Sneh's second favourite hobby

The first reason why I like baking is because I’m always hungry. Even if I’ve eaten a HUGE lunch, 30 minutes later, I become hungry again. So because of that, I need to know how to make food for myself, on my own, so I can satisfy myself. The second reason why I love cooking/baking is because it's easy. Let’s say that I want to make cookies. For that, all you need is flour, eggs, sugar, butter, vanilla, baking powder. So when I’m done baking my cookie, the final result is just amazing. The cookies taste so good, the work that you did is just worth it! Last but not least, the reason why I like cooking/baking is because it relieves stress, anxiety and it also improves your mood. Study shows that spending time in the kitchen can relief stress, because of the aroma of the sweet that you’re making. Baking is really fun, and maybe you should try it as well.
